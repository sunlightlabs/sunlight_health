Sunlight Health

Warning: The Sunlight Foundation makes no claims to be an expert on healthcare. Please confer with a medical professional and use your discretion when consulting Sunlight Health.

Sunlight Health helps medical patients and their families make informed decisions about their healthcare services and prescription drug options. The app relies on the latest data from government and private sources, so that users can be savvy healthcare consumers.

Facility and supplier data comes from the Center for Medicare and Medicaid’s Hospital Compare database and drug information comes from Consumer Reports’ Best Buy Drugs ratings. Click here for more about the data and methodology used.

Check out Sunlight’s other mobile apps, including Congress and Real Time Congress.

Sunlight Health was created by the Sunlight Foundation, a nonpartisan nonprofit that uses cutting-edge technology and ideas to make government transparent and accountable.

It was developed with generous support from the John S. and James L. Knight Foundation.
